Biography:

Sean Saville (Nazareth, Class of 2020) has studied the electric guitar since 2015. In 2017, Sean enrolled at Nazareth College for Music Business with a Jazz Studies minor. Sean is currently a member of the musical projects Free Casino, Dolce, and the Sanford Street Guitar Duo.
